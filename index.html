Helambu-Panchpokhari trekking trail

Helambu area is already developed tourism destination and quite popular for Sherpa/Hyolmo culture and lifestyle. Panchpokhari area is a emerging trekking destination which can be packaged with the Helambu. Panchpokhari area also lies at the adventure trekking route to Kyangin/Langshisa via the Till Man pass.

A preliminary feasibility study of Helambu-Panchpokhari-Chautara circuit was carried out by CEST Nepal in January 2008. Located within the eastern part of Langtang National Park (LNP) at the laps of Dorje-Lakpa Himal, this area is highly potential for tourism with its pristine forest and wild animals like Red Panda, Snow Leopard; rich Hyolmo and Tamang culture; beautiful wetland like Panch Pokhari, beautiful landscape of Melamchi and Indrabati river valley and majestic mountain views of Dorje-Lakpa and Gaurishanka Himal.

A team of explorer comprising Mr. Ram Chandra Sedai and Nabin Kumar Gupta of CEST Nepal and a local Mr. Kanchha Tamang of Bhotang VDC, Sindhupalchowk visited and explored the circuit. Up to the Bhotang VDC, a tea house trekking can be operated with some improvement on accommodation facilities with proper training and infrastructure development. After Bhotang and up to the Panchpokhari areas, some seasonal eco lodge should be established with the collective effort of Langtang National Park, VDCs, DDC Sindhupalchowk and TAAN.

Tourism development and management plan of Manakamana

On behalf of Nepal Tourism Board, CEST Nepal prepared and submitted a Tourism Development and Management Plan of Manakamana, Betini and Bakreswori areas of Gorkha district. The popular Manakamana temple is already developed as a major pilgrimage site after the operation of Cable car. Now the plan aims to attract more visitors at Manakamana areas by linking visitors with the culturally rich Betini village and naturally beautiful Bakreshwori hill. Betini village is one of the largest and beautiful Magar villages full of potential for Home-stay activities and cultural programme. Bakreshwori hill located at 1700m high in Mahabharat range is best for the scenic view of Mountain ranges comprising Annapurna range to the west and Langtang and Dorje-Lakpa Himal range to the east. Major mountain ranges closely seen from the hill are Manaslu Himal, Buddhha Himal, Ganesh Himal etc. The site is equally best located for the scenic landscape of Mahabharat range, Daraundi and Marsyangdi river. Besides natural attractions, Bakreshwori hill is also popular for Bakreshwori temple and Siddha Gufa.

Community Awareness and Tourism promotion project (CATPP), Changunarayan

CEST Nepal has been implementing a community based tourism promotion project entitled Community Awareness and Tourism Promotion Project (CATPP) at Changunarayan since February 2008. Changunarayan area is one of the world heritage Site listed sites in Kathmandu. Located at the northern-west part of Bhaktapur city, Changunarayan temple is supposed to be an oldest temple in Nepal established during the King Mandev. The temple is enriched with the presence of ten incarnations of Gods, some master piece of Asia such as Bishowrupa, Garuda and other several artifacts…
